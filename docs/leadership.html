Data Science for Executives¶

Learning objectives:

  1. Explain the essential concepts and terminology from data science.
  2. Articulate data science projects from a variety of business scenarios.
  3. Prioritize data science projects using estimated business ROI.
  4. Explain how the success of a data science project is measured.
  5. Create a plan for your business to become more data and model driven.
1 Trends Current and future trends of data science
2 Demystifying data science Demystifying Machine Learning and artificial intelligence
3 Data-driven organizations Hallmarks of data and model driven organizations
4 Data strategy Data strategy, data maturity, data governance
5 Prioritizing projects Articulating and prioritizing data science projects
6 CASE STUDY Agile product roadmaps

Note

Each unit is 2 hours in duration
