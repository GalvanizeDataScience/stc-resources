Professional Training¶

These trainings require a fundamental knowledge of Python. They trainings that are in relatively short form meant to address a specific business need. Each training is 80 hours.

Important

These are short-form trainings that are not meant to be comprehensive. However, the most industry relevant topics are included and the topic areas are covered in a way that allows students readily use the concepts and tools in their workplace.

Note

Each unit is 4 hours in duration
